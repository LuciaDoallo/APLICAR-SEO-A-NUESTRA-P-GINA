Sobre Mi.

Dennis Krawec is a Brooklyn-based designer who specializes in graphic design, art direction, photography, video and music production. With experience working for design firms, universities, and art non-profits, his work has been featured in large and small marketing campaigns both domestically and internationally. He has been recognized for his bold and unique approach to design. In his free time, you can find him listening to his favorite Kpop artists.
